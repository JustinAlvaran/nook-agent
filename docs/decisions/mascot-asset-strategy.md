# Decision: mascot asset strategy

Status: keep and harden the procedural mascot for MVP; commission/build an original rigged GLB for production.

The existing Nook is real WebGL geometry with state-driven procedural motion, color/outfit/accessory meshes, speech bubble, drag, keyboard and button movement, reduced-motion support, pause, hide, reset, and WebGL fallback. It is not a skeletal rig and must not be marketed as one.

No third-party mascot was purchased or downloaded. A stock model would create style, licensing, rig-compatibility, wardrobe, and marketplace-versioning risk. The next asset should be original and validated against `docs/mascot-production-brief.md`. Temporary third-party assets may be evaluated only under the licensing policy and may not silently become the brand mascot.
