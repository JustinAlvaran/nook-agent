# Asset licensing policy

Every non-original asset requires an asset manifest entry with source URL, creator, acquisition date, exact license/version, commercial-use rights, modification rights, attribution text, redistribution limits, proof-of-license location, and approved product surfaces. “Free download” is not a license.

Reject assets with unclear provenance, editorial-only use, no commercial rights, prohibited derivative/redistribution terms, embedded third-party trademarks, unverified AI-training/source claims, or a license incompatible with downloadable marketplace packages. Keep source files and license evidence outside public bundles when redistribution is restricted.

No third-party visual asset was added in this MVP change.
